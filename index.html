Bio

Alexander Ivanov (1950-2007) is a Russian painter born in St. Petersburg. He graduated from Mukhina art college and his early works in the late 70's include mostly tile art with surreal themes. However, since then he switched to creating landscapes, city panoramas and still lives.

Exhibition experience

Many art events and exhibitions were held and Alexander's works are featured in collections of museums in Russia, UK, Germany, Finland and in other countries. In 1980's paintings were introduced to Henry Nannen Gallery.
